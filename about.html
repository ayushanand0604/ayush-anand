ABOUT US


CITY SHADOW is an Indian Hindi-language daily newspaper that is the largest circulated daily newspaper of India. It is owned by CITY SHADOW COORPORATION, the largest Media Company of India. Started in BIHAR in 2020, it expanded AFTER 2020 with the launch of CITY SHADOW edition. Today, CITY SHADOW Group is present in INDIA in Hindi, English EDITIONS.


CITY SHADOW Media found its beginning in 2020 when its flagship newspaper, CITY SHADOW. CITY SHADOW Media is today growing day by day to become one of India’s largest media companies.


Produced by an editorial team known for its quality, innovation and integrity, CITY SHADOW (English newspaper) is the choice for nearly many readers across India, who turn to it daily for news, information, analysis and entertainment. CITY SHADOW, the group’s Hindi daily, is trying to become largest daily in the country. It enjoy a strong brand recognition among readers as well as advertisers.


This Company publishes a national business newspaper, CITY SHADOW. CITY SHADOW is a one-of-its-kind newspaper in the sense that the company has an exclusive agreement with the Wall Street Journal to publish Journal-branded news and information in India.


CITY SHADOW Media has also made its foray into electronic media. Diversifying its ambit of operations, the company in a consulting partnership with Various Organisations. Currently available in BIHAR.


Internet businesses of CITY SHADOW Media operate leading web portals WWW.CITYSHADOW.COM in the general and business news categories respectively. The company’s job portal Shine.com which has received high appreciation from consumers and industry for its innovative design and usability crossed 7 million registrations.
